About Brass Tack

Brass Tack was a group of well-travelled UX researchers and developers that offered market research and digital consulting services. The company went on indefinite hiatus in April 2014.

It was great fun while it lasted.

Over the past four years, we helped clients with communications strategy development, NPD, proof-of-concept and brand positioning studies, across a range of sectors, but mainly in higher education, healthcare, financial services (banking and insurance).

We also took on some great little UX, design and build projects for SME clients.
